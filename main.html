Custom Playlist Party Experiences for B-Side Music Lovers

About the Experience

We create unforgettable playlist parties built around theme-based music sets, blending curated playlists and live DJs with immersive artistic elements like color therapy, light shows, spoken word, dancers, and more.

Our events are designed for people who love deep cuts, hidden gems, and B-sides that deserve to shine. We bring music and community together in meaningful ways through food, drinks, and custom experiences.

How It Works

1. RSVP: Guests recommend songs when they RSVP.

2. Curate: We build a custom experience from those recommendations and our own curation.

3. Connect: We host at unique small businesses—wine bars, sandwich shops, sneaker stores—and more.

4. Travel: We bring the vibe to wherever you are—locally and on the road.
